Services

Online Lessons:

For those who are working from home or simply prefer to stay at home, Austria Language Services also offers online lessons via MS Teams and other platforms. Course content is uploaded to the MS Teams file repository and is therefore available to you at any time. This solution offers the flexibility you need when you are at home and is also extremely cost-effective.

Individual Training:

For busy executives who require flexibility in their training sessions, the most time and cost-effective solution is individual one-on-one coaching. Course content is determined together with the participant. This ensures that individual needs are optimally taken into account and rapid progress can be achieved.

Group Courses:

In contrast to individual training, where you benefit from the full attention of the trainer, group courses enable greater interaction among participants (through discussions, role plays and negotiations). Group dynamics promote participant communication, which not only enables rapid progress but also makes learning more enjoyable.

Course Content:

Austria Language Services has an extensive library of training materials, which means that almost all industry-specific topics and situations can be covered. From legislation to accounting, from information technology to HR issues, whether telephoning or email correspondence; we strive to meet all your requirements. Please note, however, that we only offer specialized English courses and not subject-specific courses in English.

Translations: ↔

All translation orders are completed by the following Monday. We also offer a "same-day service" for texts of a reasonable size. Our translators have a keen sense of the subject matter and are able to translate your texts accurately in terms of content.

About Us

Austria Language Services has been working for numerous prestigious companies in Vienna since 1999. We are not only providers of freelance language and translation services, but also trainers for presentation skills, PowerPoint training seminars and speaking techniques.

Our goal is to offer high-quality, relevant and career-oriented English lessons for adults, because we believe that communication is the key to every business success.

Whether it's simple phrases or important business negotiations or milestone negotiations: We can help you achieve your goal!

With over 20 years of practical experience, we understand your needs and requirements and can therefore optimally commit ourselves to improving your English skills.

Our translations from German into English are carried out exclusively by native English speakers. We guarantee the highest quality as our experienced translators and editors are very reliable and professional.

We cover a wide range of subject areas: real estate and property, banking and finance, HR and law, and much more. Our translations include reports, software user manuals, websites and CVs.
